Skin Solution Mapping

A simple category guide for distributors and clinic partners.

Suggested routine (AM)
  • STEP 1. Soft Balance Cleanser — gentle, low-irritation cleanse
  • STEP 2. Toner — rebalance skin pH
  • STEP 3. Hydra Glow Serum — intensive hydration & radiance
  • STEP 4. Sunscreen — UV and environmental protection
Suggested routine (PM)
  • STEP 1. First cleanse (oil/water) + Soft Balance Cleanser
  • STEP 2. Toner or essence pad
  • STEP 3. Firming Ampoule — targeted lifting & firming care
  • STEP 4. Cream or sleeping mask
By skin type
  • Dry / Dehydrated — hydrating serum + cream, consider blending with facial oils.
  • Oily / Blemish-prone — low pH cleanser + lightweight serum, non-comedogenic formulas emphasized.
  • Sensitive — fragrance / alcohol / colorant minimized, soothing actives (Cica, Panthenol, etc.) highlighted.
  • Professional Use — pre/post-procedure soothing & barrier-support lines for clinics and spas.
